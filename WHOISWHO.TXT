Description             : [en]
                          In this community project, everyone got to try out
                          the role of another mapper.
                          The aim is to create a map in the style of a random
                          mapper among other project members. Style can mean
                          different detailing techniques, commonly used
                          textures, a particular approach to implementing
                          combat, different gimmicks, highlights or any other
                          feature that can be taken from the mapper's
                          previous maps.
